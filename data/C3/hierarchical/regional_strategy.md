# Regional Strategy Notes

## Canada

Canada is usually treated as a stable market. If sales increase but profit does not improve, analysts should inspect discount levels and COGS before recommending larger campaigns.

## France

France is useful for testing midmarket and government segment performance. Revenue may look strong in some months, but the team should compare profit margin before deciding whether the campaign is healthy.

## Germany

Germany often appears across multiple segments and products. Because of this, Germany is a useful market for comparing product-level differences, especially when the same product appears in different segments.

## Mexico

Mexico is sensitive to discount strategy. When units sold are high, the team should verify whether the discount band is too aggressive and whether profit still remains positive.

## United States

The United States should be reviewed by segment before making conclusions. Enterprise, government, and small business performance can differ significantly even when total sales look similar.
